The following principles explain our vision and guide our approach in Coucou Land.
  • Protocols over platforms - if you are serious about true interoperability the standards and protocols must come first.
  • Design for Modularity - the power of Open Source is greatest when it allows for recombinative innovation.
  • Implement democratic processes - be open to alternative views
  • Promote forks - encourage different visions
  • Build communities - focus on documentation and accessibility of modular components
